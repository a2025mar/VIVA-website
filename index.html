Introduction

About VIVA Capital

VIVA Capital Management is a privately held investment firm with a long-term perspective and a disciplined approach to capital allocation. The firm invests across public and private markets, combining global insight with regional expertise in the Gulf region.

We seek opportunities where our capital, experience, and network can contribute to sustainable business performance and enduring results. VIVA Capital Management's activities span equity and credit investments, direct ownership of real assets, and partnership-driven ventures in emerging digital sectors.
